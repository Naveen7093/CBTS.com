About Us

Welcome to our College Bus Tracking System. We are dedicated to providing a reliable and efficient transportation service for students and staff. Our system allows you to track the location of buses in real-time, plan your routes, and stay updated on bus schedules. Feel free to explore the features of our system and reach out to us if you have any questions or feedback.
